Why Milwaukie Homeowners Need a Dedicated Removal Specialist

Most roofing contractors in Clackamas County will tell you they can handle the solar panels as part of your re-roof. What they won't tell you is that Oregon law requires solar removal and reinstallation to be performed by a contractor holding a valid Oregon CCB license and a state electrical license. A roofing crew that moves your panels without the right credentials puts your manufacturer warranty at risk and creates potential liability if something goes wrong with the electrical system.

We work exclusively on solar removal and reinstallation. That focus means faster turnarounds, more careful handling, and full accountability for every panel we touch. Before hiring anyone, ask directly: "What is your Oregon CCB number and can you show me your electrical license?" Any legitimate contractor answers without hesitation.

PGE Coordination for Milwaukie Homeowners

Milwaukie is in PGE territory. Your net metering agreement stays active during a temporary removal — you do not need to reapply. We notify PGE before de-energizing your system as a standard step. Net metering billing pauses during downtime and resumes automatically once the system is confirmed producing after reinstallation. You make no calls and file no paperwork.

What to Expect on a Milwaukie Solar Removal Job

  1. Pre-Job System Benchmark

    We document your system's current output before anything is touched — giving us a verified baseline to confirm after reinstallation.

  5. Reinstallation to Manufacturer Specs

    Once your roofer is done, we return and reinstall to original specifications — preserving your panel and inverter warranties.

  6. Post-Reinstall Performance Verification

    We confirm your system hits its pre-removal output before we close the job. You receive a written performance report.

Frequently Asked Questions — Milwaukie Solar Removal

We're in the Old Town Milwaukie Historic District. Does that affect our solar removal?

It can. Older homes in the historic district sometimes have non-standard roof structures, lower load ratings, and custom mounting configurations that were installed to work around the home's specific architecture. We assess these jobs individually before quoting. If we identify structural concerns or unusual configurations, we document them and walk you through our approach before any work begins.

Can you coordinate with the City of Milwaukie on permitting?

Milwaukie solar removal permits run through Clackamas County, not the City of Milwaukie directly. We submit the permit application as part of our standard process and track it through approval. You don't need to interface with the county permit office — we handle that entirely.

What if our panels are older than 15 years? Are they worth reinstalling?

That's a fair question to ask. We'll give you an honest assessment during the estimate visit. Panels older than 15 to 20 years may have degraded to the point where removal costs approach or exceed their remaining value. If that's the case, we'll tell you directly — we'd rather you make an informed decision than pay for a reinstall that doesn't make financial sense.
